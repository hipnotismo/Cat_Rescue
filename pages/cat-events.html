Work with us as a volunteer!

At Cat Rescue, our events are all about celebrating cats, bringing the community together, and supporting our mission to rescue and rehome felines in need. Whether you're looking to adopt, volunteer, learn more about feline care, or simply have a great time with fellow cat lovers, there's something for everyone! From adoption fairs and fundraising galas to fun activities like kitten yoga and cat-themed craft nights, our events help us raise awareness and provide much-needed support for our shelter. Every ticket purchased, every donation made, and every moment shared helps us save more lives.
