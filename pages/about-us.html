About Us

Welcome to Cat Rescue, a dedicated haven for cats in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray, abandoned, and at-risk cats while promoting responsible pet ownership and feline welfare. Founded in 2000, our shelter has been a safe space for countless cats, providing them with medical care, socialization, and love until they find their forever homes. We believe every cat deserves a second chance, whether they are kittens, seniors, or those with special needs. Beyond adoptions, we advocate for community awareness through trap-neuter-return (TNR) programs, educational workshops, and volunteer opportunities. Our dedicated team of staff and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every cat receives the care and compassion they deserve. At Cat Rescue, we believe that love is best measured in purrs.
